# Sleepscumming
A savescumming mod for CDDA, since people keep asking for it.

There are three versions--only use one. With the "You Lose Your Stuff" version, you...lose your stuff. 
Otherwise you don't.

With the Soulslike version (WIP), a gravestone appears where you died. Until you go interact with that gravestone, you suffer a penalty to your damage and HP. 

You have to sleep at least once for the mod to take effect.
